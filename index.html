I'm a PhD candidate in the Sagol School of Neuroscience at Tel Aviv University, advised by Prof. Yaara Yeshurun and Prof. Einat Shetreet.

My research focuses on the cognitive and neural mechanisms of belief, truth, and misinformation. I use behavioral experiments and fMRI to understand how people process (un)reliable information and how they evaluate trust in sources.

I am currently looking for postdoctoral opportunities.
